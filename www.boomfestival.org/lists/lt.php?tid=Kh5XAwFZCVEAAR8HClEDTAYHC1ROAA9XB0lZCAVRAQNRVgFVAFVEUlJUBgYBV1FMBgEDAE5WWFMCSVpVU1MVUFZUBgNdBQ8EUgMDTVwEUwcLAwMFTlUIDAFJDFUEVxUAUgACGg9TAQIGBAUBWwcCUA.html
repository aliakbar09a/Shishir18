Boom Ambassadors

Previous Next

The Boom Festival family is all around the world. 

A Boom Festival Ambassador is an individual or organisation which represents Boom Festival across the world. They sell Boom Regular and Friendly price tickets, promote the festival and can give you all the information you may need about the festival.

They are the liaison between Boom HQ and the Boomers, and are helpful brothers and sisters who do an incredible job.

They can also help you resolve any queries or questions you may have.
 
BOOM AMBASSADOR LIST

Our Ambassador List is available HERE.

Ambassador's tickets are limited and subject to availability. Tickets will be available on a first-come-first-serve basis. All tickets are personal and non-transferable. ADVANCE RESERVATIONS CANNOT BE MADE VIA BOOM AMBASSADORS.

If you do not have a Boom Ambassador in your country, then please contact the ambassador that's in the country nearest to yours, and he or she will sort out anything you may need.
Should you require any further information then please connect with us at info@boomfestival.org.
