Get involved in the making of Boom and get a whole new sense of what it means to be a Boomer!

Art is at the very core of our celebration, and Boomland is breath-taking when filled with daring, awareness-raising, surreal installations spread right across it. It shows that the imagination really knows no bounds! So whether it is through projections, sculptures or land-art, you have the opportunity to freely express your creativity in the most magical of places...

In essence, the festival is a reflection of YOU. And if you feel the vibe and want to contribute, then come and be part of it. Join us in our celebration, bring your skills to the table, and participate in this amazing project! 

Applications must be submitted no later than 28 February 2018 by 23:59 GMT. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered.
