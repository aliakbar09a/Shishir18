Boom only gets by through the active contribution of its amazing community - and you too can be a part of it! Join us in our celebration, bring your own skills to the table and actively participate in this amazing project.

There are countless ways in which you can make your contribution to Boom.

The Being Fields (formerly known as Healing Area) is paramount to the Boom experience because it serves as a peaceful and invigorating haven for everyone who wishes to rejuvenate, take time out and indulge in a series of relaxing therapies and activities.

Dedicated therapists and facilitators make a very powerful and positive contribution to this area. So if you specialise in therapies, massages, bodywork, martial arts, yoga, alternative medicines and wish to work alongside us for the next edition of Boom - then please let us know! 

As an authentic pool of knowledge, Boom is the perfect opportunity to share your abilities and learn in return. It’s only up to you to grasp it!

Applications must be submitted no later than 28 February 2018 by 23:59 GMT. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered.
