Get involved in the making of Boom and get a whole new sense of what it means to be a Boomer!

It is the incredible art and talent of the global community that makes Boom such amazing experience: in essence, the festival is a reflection of YOU. And if you feel the vibe and want to contribute, then come and be part of it. Join us in our celebration, bring your skills to the table, and participate in this amazing project! 

 

And this is why we are here: to set up and secure a loving environment that brings our favourite crowd into the heart of the Boomland. And it works, mainly because everyone is in it for the same reason - and that is to keep Boom's heart beating and to continue raising awareness through celebration. 

There are countless ways in which you can contribute. And we are sure you will be over the moon to hear that MUSIC is one of them! 

So Dj’s, bands, solo musicians, acoustic artists, please come forward! We have four music stages which play 20 hours of music each day over a total of 6 days. That’s a LOT of music. So don’t be shy, get in touch and send us your creations. The 2016 edition of Boom was a SONIC FEAST with more than 190 different music acts spread across the Dance Temple, the Alchemy Circle, The Chill Out Gardens and the Sacred Fire stages. You don't need us to tell you how the pulse of this incredible crowd feels. And if you don't know, then maybe, just maybe you are about to soon find out... So what are you waiting for? You know what to do!

Applications must be submitted no later than 28 February 2018 by 23:59 GMT. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered.
