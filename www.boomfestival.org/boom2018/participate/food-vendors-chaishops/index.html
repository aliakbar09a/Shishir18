Some  say one of the greatest things to do at Boom is to taste every single one of the yummy food stalls! Boomers are a famished crowd and our Food Area is constantly buzzing. We choose food vendors from across the world to literally represent the flavours from our planet to an international crowd.

To satisfy every appetite we like to have the most diverse selection of foods from every continent, with numerous options that include Mediterranean, Asian, vegetarian, vegan, macrobiotic, Ayurveda, gluten-free and dairy-free etc. Chai Shops are also welcome!

Our food philosophy is a conscious one, and we give priority to organic and locally, or fair-trade sourced products. It is mandatory to have biodegradable cuttlery. If you resonate with our ethos and love making people happy with good food, then this is the area for you! During our 2016 edition, 77 percent of food options at restaurants were vegetarian, and for this year, many of you may be happy to hear there will even more on offer for the 2018 edition.
