Get involved in Boom’s making and get a whole new sense of what it means to be a Boomer!

Our Flea Market is everything but typical: vendors from all over the planet set up their stalls amidst the busy roads of the Boomland to display their eccentric apparel, quirky jewellery, organic products and experimental handiwork. As Boomers love browsing through exposed merchandise, we are looking for the best shops to participate in our event. Yours could be the next one!

Boom only gets by through the active contribution of its amazing community, a community that every two years comes together to set up and secure a unique experience that brings people from the whole world into the magic Boomland.

Be a part of Boom in a very special way, pack your selling goods and help us keep Boom’s heart beating while working for raising awareness through celebration!

Applications must be sent no later then the 28th of February 2018 at 23:59 GMT. Applications sent after the deadline will not be considered.

Rental Fee

As on previous editions the renting fee for the flea market stalls for 2018 will be set per square meter (stall size), with a minimum of 12 square meters fee, adding VAT running at the time. Our fee doesn't include any worker ticket or any structure. For those that need to connect to our power grid there will be a symbolic electricity fee. Stalls located at other areas, such as Sacred Fire or Being Fields will have a reduced fee per square meter. More information regarding final rental fee will be sent for the selected ones.
Applications for fleamarket will be open from the 2nd October 2017 23:00 (GMT 0) until the 28th February 23:00 (GMT 0).

Entrance

The entrance tickets for Boom Festival 2018 for Flea Market employees are not included in the stall rental fee. If you’re selected and required previously, we will reserve tickets at the gates for the selected stalls that have not bought tickets for them and their staff team members. These tickets are exclusively for Flea Market employees, they are limited per stall and will have a fixed price of 155 euros, paid at the festival gates.
