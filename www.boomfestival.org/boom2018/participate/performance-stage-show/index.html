Boomland is an open stage! Performers from around the world meet during the full moon to honour the body as a form of art! 

It is only because of the active contribution of the global Boom community that makes Boom what it is. In essence it is a reflection of YOU! And if you can see yourself in it then perhaps you should be part of it too! 

A herd of dinasaurs stomp across the dance floor. Stunningly beautiful aliens glisten in the night. A bioflourescent fish from the deep sea is grooving to psychedelic trance. Meanwhile, in the midst of the food court, someone is seriously trying to defy the laws of gravity - and it looks like they are actually succeeding! This, dear friends, is just another day in the land of Boom... 

Magic moments like these are known very well in the world of performance art. And if you can feel at home with post-apocalyptic circus troupes, juggling loons, extraterrestrial clowns and mindblowing fire artists: then it sounds like you should be performing with them too!

So if this sounds like you, then we want to know all about what you do!

You will also be inspired to learn that last edition, we introduced a stage dedicated soley to dance performances. So we are also calling out to every passionate dancer who wishes to bring his or her moves to the dazzling Nataraja Stage!

And don't forget, there are also music stages waiting for performers like you to hypnotise dancing crowds during DJ sets and concerts. 

This is YOUR queue to come and join us for an experience like no other. Make your appearance at Boom and share your art with the most loving crowd you’ll ever meet.

Applications must be submitted no later than the 28 February 2018 by 23:59 GMT. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered.
