Young Dragons FAQ

What is Young Dragons ?

The Young Dragons’ Area is a space dedicated to the youngest Boomers and their parents. It’s an area we have prepared for children between the ages of three and 12 years.

Here, the little ones can enjoy activities which include: workshops, cinema, theatre and a host of different playgrounds. Parents and their children can share blissful moments of fun and joy, they can also rest here and be protected during the hottest hours of the day. It’s an amazing little hub for parents to also connect with each other and share their blessed experience of having a child or three!

So if you are a parent and you are bringing your little one to Boom, please take into consideration some important points which will help you to make the most of your festival experience.

REGISTERING YOUR CHILD

All children must be registered at the Boom Ticket Shop. From the 19th of October onwards, the pre-registering platform will be available.

When you buy your ticket online, please register your child in the forms provided. If you already have your ticket, be sure to also get a ticket for your child and he or she will be automatically registered.

In the registration form, please make sure you provide all the information requested. This is because it is important for us to know the age of your child so we can adapt the Boom 2018 children’s area and ensure it meets the needs of every child.

As a safety measure, it is also very important to fill out the names of emergency contacts. Please be sure to fill out this section.

 

OTHER POINTS TO CONSIDER
  • Please be mindful of the crowds and stay focused on your child/children at all times.
     
  • All children will receive a wristband upon arrival at the gates.
     
  • The wrist bands are numbered as this will help us locate parents in case a child gets lost.
     
  • There are soaring temperatures at Boom so the risk of dehydration and sunburn are high. Please be mindful of this by always keeping your children protected from the sun. Children should be wearing sun hats and given plenty of water.
     
  • Do not enter loud dance floors with your children if they’re not wearing the appropriate ear protection. You can ask for such protection at the Young Dragons Area.
     
  • Every child here must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. It is not a place to leave your children without supervision.
     
  • Last but not least: Children under the age of 13 get free admission to Boom. Hooray! However, due to extreme conditions of intense heat and dust - we strongly advice parents not to bring children under the age of three.
PARTICIPATE IN THE YOUNG DRAGON'S AREA

If you are coming to Boom and would like to contribute to the Young Dragons’ Area with your knowledge, skills and passion, please contact us at: kids@boomfestival.org.

PREGNANT BOOMERS

Babies booming in wombs are no doubt getting a vibrant start in life! We honour the sacred nature of the divine feminine to bring life into this world - it is nothing less than a sublime state of pure love - one we wish to both celebrate and help protect.

So if you happen to be pregnant, we welcome you and your unborn child with open arms! We would also like to make sure you get priority access to everything you need during your time at the festival. For health and safety purposes, it is also important to register your details so we can help you if and when you need it.

To register, please send an email to kids@boomfestival.org
