Wanbli Tipi Camp

Welcome Boomers to Wanbli Tipi Camp, the Eagle Camp, at Boom Festival 2018!

The Wanbli Camp offers you traditional accommodation at Tipis and Star Tents full equiped in a private and comfortable area during the Boom Festival 2018.

Allow your tribal side to re-emerge while staying in the camp! These natural nomadic homes remind us our connection with Mother Earth. The camp is located at a natural spot in the festival surrounded by ancient trees with beautifull views to the lake, a special quiet place to relax and chill out zone for everyone: families, friends and all those who wish to have a relaxed and unique experience at Boomland.

There are two types of acommodation services: The Whole Tent rent and Single Place at shared tent.


If you have any doubts or inquiries regarding the Wanbli Tipi Camp, please reach out to our team at: tipicamp@boomfestival.org

 

WHOLE TENT

  • Tipi for 2 pp: These are 3,5 mts tipis for two persons (couples, friends). Tipis come equipped with inner carpet and small liner (curtain) protecting the bottoms. Includes 2 single foam mattresses, bedding textiles (sheet, duvet, pillow, pillow cover, duvet cover) for 2 persons and a torch with batteries, also a keylocker to close the tent. Sizes: 10 m² surface. Inner center: 2,20 mts tall (the measurements are aproximated).

  • Star tent 2 pp: These are 3 mts tent for two persons (couples, friends). These tents come equipped with zippered groundsheet and small windows (at walls) and door closed by zipper including mosquito net.  Includes 2 single foam mattresses, bedding textiles (sheet, duvet, pillow, pillow cover, duvet cover) for 2 persons and a torch with batteries, also a keylocker to close the tent. Sizes: 7 m² surface. Inner center: 2 mts tall (the measurements are aproximated).
     
  • Star tent 5 pp: These are 5 mts tent for five persons (group of friends). These tents come equiped with zippered groundsheet and small windows (at walls) and door closed by zipper including mosquito net. Includes 5 single foam mattresses, bedding textiles (sheet, duvet, pillow, pillow cover, duvet cover) for 5 persons and a torch with batteries, also a keylocker to close the tent. Sizes: 20 m² surface. Inner center: 2,80 mts tall (the measurements are aproximated)
     
  • Tipi for 6 pp: These are 5,5 mts tipis for a group of friends. A good amount of space to dwell. These tipis come equiped with inner carpet and liner (curtain) protecting the bottoms. Includes 6 single foam mattresses, bedding textiles (sheet, duvet, pillow, pillow cover, duvet cover) for 6 persons and 2 torchs with batteries, also a keylocker to close the tents. Sizes: 24 m² surface. Inner center: 4,80 mts tall (the measurements are aproximated).

 

SINGLE PLACES
 

  • Singles Places at Shared Star tent 5 pp: This option is about sharing a tent with 4 extra people. These tents come equipped with zippered groundsheet and small windows(at walls) and door closed by zipper including mosquito net.  A single place includes a single foam mattress, bedding textiles (sheet, duvet, pillow, pillow cover, duvet cover) for 1 person and a common torch with batteries as well as a keylocker to close the tent. Sizes: 20 m² surface. Inner center: 2,80 mts tall (the measurements are aproximated).

 

THE CAMP SERVICES

The Wanbli Tipi Camp will be located in a calm, reserved area, so you, your friends and family can get a proper rest from party time. Just bring your personal belongings and love for adventure and nature. The Tipi Camp is not a VIP area, but rather a place where Boomers that request a quieter place can rest and enjoy the festival.

  • Reception: At your arrival you will find the Tipi Reception, where you can make the Check In and Check Out at the end. During the festival you will find a reception team available available 24h and a maintenance camp team happy to help you with all your tent camp doubts or needs.
     
  • Wanbli Bar: You will find a chaishop with fresh and hot drinks, toasts, sandwiches, breakfasts and more inside the camp, next to the reception area.
     
  • Chill Out: A comfortable shadow area to rest and chill next to the bar. In this area you can charge your electronic devices.
     
  • Bathroom and Showers: There will be showers and bathrooms exclusive for the camp.
     
  • Waterpoint: There will be a waterpoint where you can get drinkable water next to reception.

Stay connected to the website so you will find all the “surprises” that we prepared for the next Boom!


SPECIFICATIONS ABOUT THE CONDITIONS ON OFFER

  • Matress: The foam mattress is a  high density single mattress of 195x76x9 cm size.
     
  • Textiles: The cotton combined bedding set are the most comfortable and natural options to sleep like in your home! It is also the cleanest, as they always come clean, straight from the laundry! They come in very soft colors (lilac) to increase your relaxing while you are sleeping (sheet, duvet cover, pillow cover).
     
  • Pillow: A big size (50 x 60 cm) and soft pillow to sleep in good conditions.
     
  • Duvet: Our fresh but snug duvet (Aprox 700 gr) for the warm festival summer nights is the best option!
     
  • Torch: We give you a led torch for all the tent, aprox 100 LM, including AA batteries. If you stay at one of the big Tipis, we give you two of these torches.
     
  • Locker: We give you a locker (closed by combination in case of big tipis and big star tent, and by keys in case of two person tipis or star tent) to close the tent.

We hope to host at Wanbli Tipi Camp with love, smiles and a cozy vibe. See you at Boomand!
