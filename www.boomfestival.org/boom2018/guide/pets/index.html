Pets

DON'T LET THE DOGS OUT 

Number one rule for pet owners: If you love them - don’t bring them to Boom - animals are not allowed at the festival!

It goes without saying that Boom is an intense environment of extreme climatic conditions, lots of dust and big, boom-bastic sound system. Keep pets safe by leaving them at home with family and/or friends.

DOGS DON'T LIKE LOUD MUSIC 

Dogs can detect sounds in the frequency range of approx. 67-45,000Hz. Compare this to humans who hear within the frequency of 64-23,000Hz. It means they can hear sounds we cannot. So can you imagine what it would be like for them to hear the bass-lines of a dark forest track? We doubt your dog will show any appreciation for hi-tech either!

BE RESPONSIBLE

Animals are beautiful because of their ability to love us so unconditionally. They provide mental, emotional, physical benefits, stress relief, entertainment, companionship and comfort. They can relieve depression, anxiety and even physical pain. They hold up their side of the bargain with all this. So we must too by being responsible pet owners.

DEADLY DOG DISEASE

Temperatures across Boomland can soar up to 40 degree celsius. But it doesn’t stop there. In this region of the Iberian Peninsula, there are high rates of leishmaniasis - an illness which causes extreme pain and is deadly to pets.

Furthermore, the subspecies of the parasite leismania donovani can infect dogs, causing a form of disease called visceral leismaniasis. Dogs serve as a reservoir of infection because the disease is transmitted between them and to people by sandflies.

ABANDONED DOGS ATTACKING BOOMERS

Whether you meant to do it or not - it is NOT COOL to abandon your dog. At previous editions of Boom, there have been many cases of abandoned dogs, leading to incidents where fellow Boomers were attacked by pets left alone and in stress. It’s not clever to bring your dog to Boom.

DON'T TAKE THE RISK

If you bring your dog to boom and get caught, don’t take it personally when we escort you out of the festival.

CARAVAN PARK B: THE ONLY PLACE YOU CAN BRING YOUR DOG 

You can bring your dog to Boom but only if you stay in Caravan Park B and have the right conditions to keep you pet there. When staying in Caravan Park B with your dog, make sure you keep your dog on a leash to prevent dog fights or attacks.
